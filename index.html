Our Commitment

Building a Sustainable Future

At Majid Rizq General Hardware, sustainability is a core business value. We are committed to responsible sourcing, waste reduction, and supporting local communities across Uganda.

Responsible Sourcing

We partner with certified suppliers who adhere to ethical and environmental standards, ensuring our steel and materials are responsibly produced.

Waste Reduction

Our operations are designed to minimize waste. We recycle offcuts and packaging materials, working to reduce our carbon footprint at every step.

Community Support

We actively invest in local communities through employment, training programs, and supporting small construction businesses across Uganda.
